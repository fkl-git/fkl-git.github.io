About

I am a student at UWC Thailand with a strong interest in law, global governance, and the structures that shape international justice.

My work explores how institutions claim legitimacy and where those claims begin to fracture—particularly in the context of international law and selective enforcement.

Beyond academics, I have been involved in leadership, research, and community initiatives that emphasize communication, strategy, and problem-solving.

I am currently developing my interests through writing, independent research, and academic competitions.
