How to Use This Well

01

Start with the exact question. A vague question will produce a vague argument.

02

Use the claim as a direct answer, not a topic. Avoid writing only what the essay is “about.”

03

The warrant is where the logic lives. Explain the mechanism connecting the claim to the evidence.

04

Evaluation should make a judgement. It should explain limits, conditions, trade-offs, and relative strength.
